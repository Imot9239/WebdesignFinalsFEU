Desserts

Halo-halo

A staple dessert made in the Philippines. It consists of plenty of different dishes mixed with the main dish of ube ice cream that works well all together.

Buchi

Buchi, also known as sesame balls, are golden, chewy, and extra delicious with sweetened red bean filling. Delicious as a snack or dessert!.

White Halo-halo

Same as the usual halo-halo but this time it is paired with vanilla ice cream and leche flan with cheese and cereal crisps for an even more in-depth and sweeter flavor.

Choco Pao

A dessert that resembles a donut that has a chocolate filling. Recommended after eating your fill of Chowking food!
